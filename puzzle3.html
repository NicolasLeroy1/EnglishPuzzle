The strange dream

You are quite knowledgeable, but are you smart ?

"There are 3 strange tribes on my island , and each morning , the following events happen simultaneously : Each person of the blue tribe becomes two of the red tribe. Half of the red tribe becomes blue , and half becomes yellow. A quarter of the yellow tribe disapears into thin air. Yet each morning , each tribe is as numerous as the day before. What is the population of each tribe ? "
